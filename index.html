WAR

Welcome to the game of WAR!

  • This is the same game you remember from your childhood!
  • The player with the highest card takes the cards from that round.
  • If there is a tie between the highest value cards, then we have WAR!
  • When there is WAR, each player puts a card into the pot, and then shows their next card. The player with the highest card showing wins the cards.
  • Select the 'Number of Players' and press 'Start New Game' to begin.
  • Good Luck!
